          Create Application Inventory for EC2 instance

1- Open SSM
2- Under instances and Nodes, select "Inventory" link
3- Click the "Setup Inventory" botton
4- Provide a name like "Webserver Inventory"
5- Manually select your EC2 instance
6- Select all categories you want to include in your inventory
7- Click "Setup Inventory"
8- Click on view details in the green bar "setup inventory successfully"
9- Click on "Apply Assocication Now"
10- Status will now show as pending
11- After the status changes to "Success", click the link
12- Go to the "Resources" tab
13- Click on "ResourceID"
14- Click on "Invnetory" tab to view a list of applications installed on the instance.
